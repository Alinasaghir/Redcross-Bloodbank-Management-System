Blood Donation Campaigns

We are going to conduct blood donation campaigns at the following places on 2-December-2023


NED University of Engg & Technology

We are excited to announce the start of a blood drive in NED University. Our aim is to encourage students and faculty members to donate blood and help meet the increasing demand for blood in hospitals.






Main Shahra-e-Faisal, Sindhi Muslim

A blood drive is starting in Sindhi Muslim Society near Main Shahra-e-Faisal. Join us in donating blood and saving lives in the community.This initiative will help to address the shortage of blood in hospitals.





Malir Flyover, Darakshan society

A blood drive will be started at Malir Flyover, an initiative aimed at addressing the shortage of blood in the area. We invite all eligible donors to join us in this noble cause and help save lives in the community. Let's make a difference together.
