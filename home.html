Who Are We?

RedCross Blood Bank is a non-profit healthcare organization, serving the community by providing high quality affordable medical services in Pakistan.Started in 1979, RedCross Blood Bank was formed with the aim to provide safe blood to our communities. The vision of RedCross has since grown to become a holistic healthcare services organization. In line with this vision, the service offerings have expanded into multiple distinct divisions.

The functions of RedCross Blood Bank now includes Blood Donation from people and from Hospital Organizations and it also provides blood to both of them if they request as blood recepient.We appreciate your valuable feedback and will do more than expected to improve our services to you.RedCross Blood Bank has only one motto and that is

"The sole meaning of life is to serve humanity"
